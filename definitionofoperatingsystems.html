An operating system is a computer system program designed to provide an operating platform for a group of other programs, and is responsible for managing parts, software, and resources of computer hardware. The intermediary operating system between the user and the computer is the operating system, which is the primary part responsible for operating the various user programs, and the use of operating systems is not limited to Computers only, these systems are used in a different set of other smart devices such as mobile phones, video games, web servers, and supercomputers, and computer operating systems are specialized in carrying out the identification of input elements that are made through the keyboard, and sending outputs Various to the display screen to be displayed to the user.In addition to that, operating systems and their types are concerned with tracking the special paths in files and their types, the information that is saved on different storage media, and controlling the peripheral parts of the computer such as the mouse and keyboard. The Microsoft Windows operating system, which is specialized in operating personal computers and desktop systems, is one of the most important Operating systems used. Operating systems and their types are among the most important elements of configuring computers and smart devices as the primary component and platform through which programs and applications are used in various fields. s
